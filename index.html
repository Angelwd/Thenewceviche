The Restaurant

We are a family-owned and operated restaurant, located in the heart of downtown Isla Verde. We specialize in authentic Peruvian food that is both delicious and nutritious. Our food is made with love from the best ingredients, served by caring staff who will make sure you have the best experience possible.
